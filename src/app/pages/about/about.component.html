Our apples are grown in the pristine Himalayan valleys of Kinnaur

Nestled at 10,000 feet above sea level, our orchards benefit from pure mountain air and natural spring water. The unique climate creates apples with exceptional sweetness and crunch.

Our traditional methods ensure pesticide-free cultivation. Each apple is handpicked at peak ripeness and delivered farm-fresh to your doorstep.
